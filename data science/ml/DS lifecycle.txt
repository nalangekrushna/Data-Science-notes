Data Science Life cycle

1) understand business requirements = define the problem and customer. product manager
2) data acquitision  = ETL = sql,log files and hadoop. data engineer
3) data preparation = cleaning,pre-processing. 
4) Exploratory Data Analysis ,hypothesis testing.slice and dice data
5) Modelling Evaluation and Interpretation
6) communicate the results.
7) deployment    software engineer, distributed system engineer
8) real time testing, A/B testing 
9) customer business buy in
10) operations = retrain model, handle failures, procedure for failures and retraining.
11) optimizations = improve model, more data, more features, optimize code.
