Different ways to deal with imbalanced data
	1) oversampling
	2) undersampling
	3) K means clustering
	4) SMOTE 
	5) MSMOTE
	6) ensembles or hybrid
	
Metrics to apply in imbalanced datasets
	1) precision/specificity
	2) recall / sensitivity
	3) f1 score
	4) MCC : correlation coef between observed and predicted binary classification
	5) Area Under Curve
